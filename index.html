About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization working towards building a better society through impactful social initiatives. The foundation focuses on education, environmental sustainability, food distribution, women empowerment, and animal welfare.

Our Projects

📚 Project Bachpanshala

Supporting underprivileged children with quality education and learning opportunities.

🌱 Project Prakriti

Tree plantation and environmental awareness campaigns for a greener future.

🍽️ Project SEVA

Food distribution drives helping needy communities and homeless people.

🐾 Project JEEV

Animal feeding and welfare initiatives supporting stray animals.

👩 Project Udaan

Women empowerment and skill development initiatives for self-reliance.

📈 Project Vikas

Community development and social growth programs for sustainable impact.
